FAQs

What is the GCL?

The GCL is the Glassboro-Camden-Line, a light rail service that will reinstate the the rail line that once helped build our communities and connect us to economic hubs of Philadelphia and local universities.

Who will ride the GCL?

  • Busy Moms

    Need to entertain the kids but don't want to spend all that cash on tolls and parking? Hop on the GCL and take just one transfer to get into Center City Philadelphia, where there's a musuem for everyone.

  • Career Professionals

    People go where the jobs are. With the growing industry in Camden and abundance of services in Philadelphia, the GCL will help attract those big paychecks back into our communities. Young professionals are more likely to stick around and raise families here when they have easy access to good jobs.

  • Sports Fans

    South Jersey loves some Philly sports, but not the traffic. Get to your favorite stadium without the hassle, just easy walk down the block.

  • College Students

    Glassboro has seen explosive growth in recent years due to the growth of Rowan University, and that's not slowing down anytime soon. Rowan commuter traffics causes huge back ups all over, especially on Rt 55. The GCL will eliminate that traffic, while also bringing bored college students with Boro Bucks to spend in our local businesses. Meanwhile, the rest of the community benefits from easier access to Cooper Medical School and the expanded Rowan-Rutgers community

  • Savvy Shoppers & Hungry Tummies

    Ever wanted to support local businesses, but got scared away due to your inability to parallel park? Not anymore! All of our Mom & Pop shops from the entire stretch of the corridor can now see Wenonah families happily dining in Pitman resturants. Get yourself that Jersey Fresh artisan honey without even having to get in the car!
